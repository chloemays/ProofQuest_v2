❖ The Royal Sage ❖

Classroom Walkthrough & Tutorial

Welcome to The Chronicles of Euclid! This interactive Geometry Proof Quest helps students master geometric proofs through an engaging, medieval narrative. Students act as the Royal Heir to the Kingdom of Euclid — a realm afflicted by "Logical Decay." To rebuild bridges, castles, and farmlands, students must complete two-column proofs.

Realm crests: ⚜ Isocele (Levels 1–10) · ◇ Rhombic Sands (11–15) · ❀ Gaelic Grids (16–20).

❖ Common Core State Standards (CCSS) Aligned ❖
  • HSG.CO.C.9: Prove theorems about lines and angles. (Levels 4, 10, 13, 16)
  • HSG.CO.C.10: Prove theorems about triangles. (Levels 1–9, 11–12, 14–18, 20)
  • HSG.CO.C.11: Prove theorems about parallelograms. (Levels 11, 14, 15)
  • HSG.SRT.B.4: Prove theorems about similarity. (Level 19)
  • HSG.SRT.B.5: Use congruence and similarity criteria for triangles. (Levels 1–8, 11–12, 14–17, 19, 20)

Premium Access: The first 5 levels are free. To unlock the remaining 15 levels, provide students with the password: MathI5Fun.
